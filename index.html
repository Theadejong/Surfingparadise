Surfers Paradise

Meet John

John got lost while surfing with his mates and needs to return home safely. The only way for John to get back home is to ride out the wave and avoid any sharks who would love to take a tasty bite out of John. Avoid the sharks and along the way collect as many Hawaiian Super Power Flowers as possible.

Instructions

  • If John gets bitten by a shark: game-over,
    the Shark had a nice meal!
  • Use your keypad ↕ & ↔ to help John surfing and collect as many Hawaiian flowers as possible along the ride.
  • Collect Hawaiian flowers to increase his powers. John believes the flowers have super powers (superstition).
